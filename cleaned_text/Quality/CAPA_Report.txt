Ltd. - Internal Document Corrective and Preventive Action (CAPA) Report CAPA Reference: CAPA-2026-002 | Related Defect: DEF-003 | Owner: Anjali Thomas Problem Statement Incoming high-brightness LED chips from KeralaLED Components (SUP-01) batch received under GRN-4012 showed luminous output readings below the rated 100-110 lm specification, affecting 320 units. Root Cause Investigation identified a binning inconsistency at the supplier's chip-sorting stage following a recent production-lot changeover. Corrective Action Affected batch quarantined and returned to supplier via Return Note RN-014. Replacement batch requested with an additional pre-shipment luminous-output test certificate. Preventive Action Supplier required to submit a pre-shipment Certificate of Conformance including luminous-output test results for all future shipments. Incoming Inspection SOP updated to include a luminous-output spot-check for LED chip receipts. Status Closed - verified effective over next 3 shipments with zero luminous-output deviations.
