VOLTORA Manufacturing Pvt. Ltd. - Internal Document VOLTORA Manufacturing Pvt. Ltd. Company Profile & Overview VOLTORA Manufacturing Pvt. Ltd. is a small and medium enterprise (SME) manufacturer of standardized LED lighting, power electronics and IoT-enabled electronic products, headquartered at Plot 27, Kalamassery Industrial Estate, Kochi, Kerala 683104, India. Incorporated in 2022, the company runs a lean team of 12 employees across one production unit and one warehouse, both in Kochi, Kerala. GSTIN: 32ABCDE7890F1Z6. Contact: +91 484 271 5540, info@voltora.in. Vision To become Kerala's most trusted small-scale manufacturer of dependable, affordable electronic products for everyday homes, businesses and public infrastructure. Mission VOLTORA Manufacturing exists to design and produce reliable LED lighting, power electronics and smart IoT devices at fair prices, supporting local dealers and distributors while maintaining strict quality standards - all within the disciplined budget of a lean, 10-12 person manufacturing team. Business Overview VOLTORA manufactures 10 standardized product SKUs across 3 categories - LED Lighting, Power Electronics and IoT & Automation - sold through dealers, distributors, online resellers and institutional/government buyers across Kerala and select other states. Annual turnover for FY Jul-2025 to Jun-2026 was approximately Rs. 29,385,530.00, with sales showing clear seasonal peaks around Onam, Christmas/New Year and Vishu. The company sources components from 10 approved suppliers and operates from a single production-unit-and-warehouse pair in Kochi, keeping logistics and overheads lean.
