Scope of Supply PowerCell Battery Traders shall supply Lithium-ion Battery Cells to VOLTORA Manufacturing Pvt. Ltd. on an ongoing basis as per periodic Purchase Orders issued under this contract. 2. Pricing Prices are fixed for the contract term subject to a maximum quarterly escalation of 3%, applicable only on documented component cost increases and subject to mutual written agreement. Given VOLTORA's SME scale, order volumes per PO are typically smaller and more frequent. 3. Lead Time & Delivery Standard lead time is 12 calendar days from PO acceptance. Late delivery beyond 3 days without prior notice is subject to a penalty of 0.5% of order value per day, capped at 5%. 4. Payment Terms Net 45 from the date of GRN acceptance. 5. Quality Requirements Supplier shall maintain ISO 9001:2015 certification and provide a Certificate of Conformance with every shipment. Defect rate must not exceed 1% of quantity supplied. 6. Termination Either party may terminate this agreement with 60 days written notice. VOLTORA may terminate immediately for repeated quality non-conformance or supply failure.
