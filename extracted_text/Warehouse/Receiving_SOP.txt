Receiving Standard Operating Procedure
Document No: SOP-WH-02 | Effective Date: 01-Jul-2025
Procedure Steps
•
Step 1: Verify inbound vehicle against the scheduled Purchase Order and Delivery Schedule.
•
Step 2: Unload goods to the Receiving Dock and match quantity against the supplier's Delivery Challan.
•
Step 3: Perform Incoming Inspection per the Incoming Inspection SOP - check for damage and verify
quality certificate.
•
Step 4: Record accepted quantity on a Goods Receipt Note (GRN) referencing the PO number.
•
Step 5: Route rejected material to the Quarantine Area and initiate a Return Note if applicable.
•
Step 6: Update the stock register with put-away bin location for accepted stock within 30 minutes.
