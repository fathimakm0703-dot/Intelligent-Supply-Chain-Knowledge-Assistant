Picking Standard Operating Procedure
Document No: SOP-WH-03 | Effective Date: 01-Jul-2025
Procedure Steps
•
Step 1: Picker receives a pick list sorted by bin location sequence for the Sales Order.
•
Step 2: Pick items using FIFO, verifying product ID against the pick list.
•
Step 3: Update the stock register to decrement system stock for each item picked.
•
Step 4: Stage picked items at the Packing Area for the corresponding Sales Order.
•
Step 5: Report short-picks or damaged items immediately to the Warehouse & Logistics Executive.
