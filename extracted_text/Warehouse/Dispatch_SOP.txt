Dispatch Standard Operating Procedure
Document No: SOP-WH-05 | Effective Date: 01-Jul-2025
Procedure Steps
•
Step 1: Confirm vehicle arrival against the Delivery Schedule and verify driver/vehicle details.
•
Step 2: Load packages in reverse delivery-route sequence to ease multi-stop unloading.
•
Step 3: Generate a Delivery Challan and Invoice for each Sales Order being dispatched.
•
Step 4: Obtain driver signature confirming quantity loaded; update Shipment Tracking Report.
•
Step 5: Notify customer of dispatch with expected delivery date.
