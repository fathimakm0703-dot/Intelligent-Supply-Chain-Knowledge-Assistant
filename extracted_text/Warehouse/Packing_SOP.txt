Packing Standard Operating Procedure
Document No: SOP-WH-04 | Effective Date: 01-Jul-2025
Procedure Steps
•
Step 1: Verify picked items against the Sales Order and generate a Packing List.
•
Step 2: Pack items using approved cartons, ensuring PCB-based items (IoT modules, smart switches) are
cushioned against static discharge and impact.
•
Step 3: Affix the Packing List and shipping label showing Sales Order number, customer, and destination.
•
Step 4: Weigh and record final package weight for freight costing.
•
Step 5: Move packed order to Dispatch Staging.
