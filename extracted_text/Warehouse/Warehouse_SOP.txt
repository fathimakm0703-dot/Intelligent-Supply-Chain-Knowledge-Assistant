Warehouse Standard Operating Procedure
Document No: SOP-WH-01 | Effective Date: 01-Jul-2025 | Version 1.0
1. Purpose
This SOP defines standard procedures for receiving, put-away, storage, picking, packing and dispatch
operations at VOLTORA's single warehouse in Edappally, Kochi.
2. Warehouse Zones
Zone
Purpose
Receiving Dock
Inbound goods inspection and staging
Bulk Storage
Pallet-level storage of high-volume SKUs (LED bulbs, tube lights, chargers)
Pick Face
Fast-moving small items for order picking
Packing Area
Order consolidation and packing
Dispatch Dock
Outbound staging and vehicle loading
Quarantine Area
Rejected or hold material pending disposition
3. General Rules
All movements must be recorded in the stock register within 30 minutes of physical completion.
FIFO (First-In-First-Out) is followed for all SKUs unless otherwise specified.
Only trained personnel may operate the warehouse's hand pallet trucks and packing equipment.
