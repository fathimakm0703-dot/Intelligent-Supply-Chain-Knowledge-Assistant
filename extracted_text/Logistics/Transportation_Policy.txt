On-Time Delivery Target
VOLTORA targets a 93% On-Time-In-Full (OTIF) delivery rate, measured monthly and reported in the OTIF
Report.
Cost Control
As an SME operating on a lower logistics budget, freight costs are tracked per route and reviewed monthly in
the Freight Cost Report to identify consolidation opportunities and avoid part-load shipments wherever possible.
