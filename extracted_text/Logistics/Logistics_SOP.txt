Logistics Standard Operating Procedure
Document No: SOP-LOG-01 | Effective Date: 01-Jul-2025
1. Purpose
This SOP governs outbound transportation planning, vehicle scheduling and shipment tracking for deliveries
from VOLTORA's Edappally warehouse to customers across Kerala and neighboring states.
2. Route Planning
Delivery routes are planned weekly based on the Delivery Schedule, consolidating orders by destination region
to maximize vehicle fill rate and minimize freight cost per unit - important given VOLTORA's limited logistics
budget.
3. Vehicle Allocation
Vehicles are allocated based on order volume/weight against VOLTORA's small in-house fleet of 1 delivery
vehicle, per the Vehicle Details register. Third-party transporters are engaged for inter-state deliveries and
routes exceeding in-house fleet capacity.
4. Shipment Tracking
All dispatched shipments are tracked using the Shipment Tracking Report, updated at each checkpoint
(dispatch, in-transit, delivered) and shared with the Logistics & Warehouse Executive.
5. Proof of Delivery
Drivers must obtain a signed Delivery Challan from the customer as proof of delivery, which is filed against the
corresponding Sales Order and Invoice.
