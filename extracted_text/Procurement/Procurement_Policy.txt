Procurement Policy
Document No: POL-PROC-01 | Effective Date: 01-Jul-2025 | Version 1.2
1. Purpose
This policy defines the rules and approval workflow governing the purchase of electronic components and
services for VOLTORA Manufacturing Pvt. Ltd., a small and medium enterprise (SME) manufacturer of LED
lighting, power electronics and IoT devices. It ensures purchases are made from approved suppliers at
competitive prices, on time, and within the company's limited operating budget.
2. Scope
This policy applies to all procurement of components (LED chips, PCBs, battery cells, plastic enclosures,
transformers, chips, solar panels, relays, connectors) and packaging materials for VOLTORA's single
production unit and warehouse in Kochi.
3. Supplier Approval
All suppliers must be listed on the Approved Vendor List (AVL) before a Purchase Order can be raised.
New suppliers require a Vendor Evaluation Report scoring a minimum of 3.5/5 across quality, delivery, pricing
and compliance criteria before onboarding.
Given the company's lean team, supplier evaluation is conducted jointly by the Procurement & Inventory
Executive and the Managing Director.

5. RFQ & Quotation Process
For purchases above Rs. 50,000, a Request for Quotation (RFQ) must be issued to a minimum of two approved
suppliers. Quotations are compared on a Quotation Comparison Sheet covering unit price, lead time, payment
terms and quality certification before award.
6. Purchase Order Terms
All Purchase Orders (PO) must specify material, quantity, unit price, delivery date and delivery location.
Standard payment terms follow the individual Supplier Contract, typically Net 15 to Net 45.
As a low-budget SME, VOLTORA favors smaller, more frequent order quantities to minimize working capital
tied up in inventory.

7. Goods Receipt & Quality Acceptance
Components are accepted only after Incoming Inspection per the Incoming Inspection SOP and recorded on a
Goods Receipt Note (GRN). Rejected material is returned to the supplier using a Return Note and logged in the
Defect Report.
