1. Quality Policy
VOLTORA Manufacturing Pvt. Ltd., a small and medium enterprise (SME), is committed to manufacturing
standardized LED lighting, power electronics and IoT-enabled products that consistently meet customer and
regulatory requirements through continuous improvement, defect prevention and supplier quality management -
within the company's limited operating budget.
2. Quality Management System
The company maintains an ISO 9001:2015 certified Quality Management System covering incoming inspection,
in-process control, final inspection and corrective/preventive action (CAPA) processes, administered by a single
Quality Assurance Executive given the company's lean 12-person team.
3. Incoming Quality Control
All components (LED chips, PCBs, batteries, transformers, chips, solar panels, relays) are inspected per the
Incoming Inspection SOP prior to acceptance into inventory.
4. In-Process Quality Control
Production work orders include mandatory quality checkpoints defined in the Routing Sheet, verified by the
Production Supervisor at each stage.
5. Final Inspection
Finished goods undergo final inspection and functional testing per documented product specifications before
release to finished-goods inventory.
6. Non-Conformance & CAPA
Non-conforming material or process is logged in the Defect Report. Defects affecting more than 1% of a batch
trigger a mandatory CAPA Report.
7. Supplier Quality
Suppliers are evaluated per the Vendor Evaluation Report and Supplier Performance Report, with a minimum
defect-rate requirement of 1% specified in Supplier Contracts.
