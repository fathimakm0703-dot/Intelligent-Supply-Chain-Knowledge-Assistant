Incoming Inspection Standard Operating Procedure
Document No: SOP-QA-01 | Effective Date: 01-Jul-2025
Procedure Steps
•
Step 1: Sample material per AQL Level II sampling plan (IS 2500 / ISO 2859-1), scaled to VOLTORA's
smaller batch sizes.
•
Step 2: Verify component specifications (electrical ratings, dimensions) against the approved datasheet.
•
Step 3: Check for physical damage, ESD exposure or contamination.
•
Step 4: Verify supplier Certificate of Conformance and batch/lot traceability.
•
Step 5: Record results on the Final Inspection Report / Defect Report as applicable.
•
Step 6: Accept, reject or place material on hold in the Quarantine Area based on inspection outcome.
